TEAMSTERS LOCAL 2024

LOGO CONTEST

Your design. Our future. Be a part of history.

Calling all Teamsters of Local 2024! We need a bold, inspiring logo that reflects our union’s dedication to better healthcare, member support, and professional excellence.

Submit your design and be a part of shaping the future of our local! This is your chance to create a meaningful symbol that represents our strength, unity, and commitment to all nursing professionals.

  • Deadline: March 31, 2025, 11:59 PM
  • Eligibility: All members of Teamsters Local 2024
  • Requirements: Incorporate both healthcare & Teamsters elements

Ready to submit? Send us your design along with the requested details. We look forward to seeing your creative ideas!

Submit Logo

All contestants will remain anonymous throughout the contest. The winning logo design will be selected strictly based on the majority of votes. The winner’s name will only be announced during the official presentation of Local 2024’s new logo, unless they choose to remain anonymous.

The selected logo design will be used on merchandise, promotional materials, and official union branding. By submitting a design, you confirm that it is copyright-free and you grant Teamsters Local 2024 full rights to use, modify, and distribute the design as needed.
